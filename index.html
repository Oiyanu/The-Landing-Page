THE MAZE

My inspiration for developing Maze-Game stemmed from an interest in game development. I was eager to explore new methods and techniques applicable to software development. This led me to study the Raycasting tool and use the SDL2 library to create the game. It was fascinating to learn how early games, like Wolfenstein 3D, achieved a pseudo-3D visual effect, revolutionizing the gaming industry and paving the way for other game engines and technologies.
